The Recruitment Process Flow

  1. Profile Setup & Resume Analysis: Students build profiles and get readiness optimization suggestions.
  2. Opening Recruitment Drives: Visiting companies register drives and specify branch & GPA limits.
  3. Candidate Application Routing: Eligible applicants submit applications which are routed to recruiter vetting dashboards.
  1. Interview Scheduling: Coordinators shortlist candidates and schedule coding/technical rounds.
  2. Offer Dispatch & Acceptance: Selected students receive placement offers and accept them.

Frequently Asked Questions

What criteria determines student eligibility for drives? +
Students must maintain a minimum cumulative CGPA of 6.0 and have no active backlogs. However, specific companies may dictate higher CGPA bounds (e.g. 8.5 CGPA required for top-tier software roles), which are filtered automatically by the system.
How are interview schedules synchronized? +
Once a coordinator shortlists a candidate, they schedule the interview block on the admin console. This updates the student dashboard application timeline immediately with the date, time, and round information.
Can recruiters directly access student resumes? +
Yes, companies registered and approved by the TPC Admin Hub can browse applicant credentials, view student skills, and see the optimized resume score index.
